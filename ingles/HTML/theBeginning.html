My story with coding

My journey into programming began with an early desire to learn, even at the age of 3, in Salvador. Although I could barely speak, I yearned for a computer course at Microlins. At the age of 8, we moved to São Gonçalo dos Campos for health reasons, but my passion for computers remained latent.

Now, jumping to the pandemic scenario, I came across the GrassHopper app, which promised to teach programming. Excitedly, I began to learn, but soon realized I wasn't grasping much. Then, BYJU'S Future School appeared on my radar through incessant advertisements. This was the turning point (note: it was the end of 2021).

Although I initially kept my desire to learn programming a secret, my passion grew with every BYJU'S advertisement. Finally, I confided in my father, and together we researched and explored the possibility. After a trial lesson, my fascination was confirmed. I was enchanted, and so were my parents, and since then, I've been at BYJU'S, eager to explore the world of programming.

Take a look at the projects I have in the "Projects" section. My passion for programming only continues to grow, and I'm excited about what the future holds in this thrilling learning journey.
