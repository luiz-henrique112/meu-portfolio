My story with coding

My journey into programming began with an early desire to learn, even at the age of 3, in Salvador. Even though I could hardly speak, I longed for a computer course at Microlins. At the age of 8, we moved to São Gonçalo dos Campos for health reasons, but my passion for computers remained latent.
Now, fast forward to the pandemic scenario, I stumbled upon the GrassHopper app, which promised to teach programming. Excited, I started learning, but soon realized I wasn't understanding anything. Then, BYJU'S Future School came on my radar through relentless advertisements. That was the turning point (note: it was the end of 2021).
Although I initially kept my desire to learn programming a secret, my passion grew with every BYJU'S ad. Finally, I told my father, and together we explored the possibility. After a trial class, my fascination was confirmed. I was delighted, and so were my parents, and since then, I've been with BYJU'S, eager to explore the world of programming.
Take a look at the projects I have in the "Projects" section. My passion for programming continues to grow, and I'm excited about what the future holds in this exciting learning journey.
